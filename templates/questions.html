Infosys 2015-09-07

  1. A farmer travelled a distance of 61 km in 9 hours. He travelled partly on foot @ 4 km/hr and partly on bicycle @ 9 km/hr. The distance travelled on foot is:
    2. 14 km
    15 km
    16 km
    17 km
    Explination:
    Speed = 600 m/sec. 5 x 60 = 2 m/sec. Converting m/sec to km/hr (see important formulas section) = 2 x 18 km/hr 5 = 7.2 km/hr.
